:wxdt:`API 实现差异 <notsupport>`
=======================================

由于运行环境的差异，下列 API 在开发者工具的实现与客户端不一致，请使用真机调试

+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
|                     API                      |         说明         |
+==============================================+======================+
| :func:`wx.checkIsSupportSoterAuthentication` | 设备不支持           |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.startSoterAuthentication`          | 设备不支持           |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.chooseInvoiceTitle`                | 模拟数据返回         |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.addPhoneContact`                   | 设备不支持           |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.onUserCaptureScreen`               | 设备不支持           |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.startBeaconDiscovery`              | 设备不支持           |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.stopBeaconDiscovery`               | 设备不支持           |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.getBeacons`                        | 设备不支持           |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.onBeaconUpdate`                    | 设备不支持           |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.onBeaconServiceChange`             | 设备不支持           |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.makePhoneCall`                     | 弹窗提示接口调用成功 |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.onCompassChange`                   | 设备不支持           |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
| :func:`wx.chooseAddress`                     | 模拟数据返回         |
+----------------------------------------------+----------------------+
